History

Haldia Institute Of Technology-the sanctum sanctorum of brilliant ideas and far sighted dreams have yielded a number of students over the year, who have managed to leave an undelible mark ,not only in the college campus but also in the society. NEEDS- an organization based in Haldia, West Bengal is one such embodiment of humanitarian beliefs latent within a very special student of the academic year 2009, of this very institution. A regular visitor of the nearby tea stalls, so called ‘theks’, once he noticed young boys working to earn their livelihood in the dingy atmosphere. This forced him to ponder over the heinous situation of child labour prompting him to work towards the welfare of these little children. It started small scale with the distribution of chocolates, books, stationary, warm clothes , and mainly basic sustaining articles that lighted up their small world. With the lapse of time, other students of this institution felt inspired to carry out such philanthropic activities which gave birth to the organization of NEEDS. The organization was finally officially inaugurated in the year 2013 on 14th November mirroring the occasion of Children’s Day.

Vision & Mission

The mission of this organization is to help promote sustainability and self sufficiency. The ultimate ambition of this organisation is to spread a smile and to lend a hand for the betterment of the children of this society. The basic aim of NEEDS is to bring about a positive change in the society through self sufficiency and the promotion of literacy. Its aim is to help promote the abolishment of child labour, and other heinous practices and evil social calamities that act as a hindrance to the healthy upbringing of children irrespective of their backgrounds.
